What's my interest?

Research Topics

The research topics I am currently working on, or have recently worked on, include:

  • Neuro-symbolic AI and XAI: exploring hybrid AI models that combine symbolic reasoning with machine learning to enhance interpretability and explainability.
  • Knowledge engineering: developing rule-based (expert) systems to organize, capture, and apply domain knowledge.
  • Conceptual representation: investigating methods for representing complex concepts and relationships in a structured manner to support AI systems.
  • Inference processes and qualitative reasoning: focused on building systems that can infer new knowledge and reason about qualitative relationships in complex domains.
  • Discrete event simulation: using simulation models to represent and analyze the behavior of systems (neural networks) over time through discrete events.

These research areas have been applied in various fields, including Industry 4.0 and Medical domains, where they contribute to improving automation, diagnostics, and decision-making processes.
