What's my interest?

Research Topics

Research topics that I'm working on, or have recently worked on, include:

Knowledge Engineering: My main research interests are focused in Knowledge Engineering, Ontological Engineering and Model-based Reasoning. More precisely, I am interested in the areas of Knowledge Modelling (including conceptualization, formalization and implementation), Ontology Development, Ontology Evaluation, and Ontology Design Patterns.

Descrete Event Modeling and Simulation: In this area, my research focus on discrete-event simulation (DES) to model and simulate the behavior complex systems as a (discrete) sequence of events in time. Each event occurs at a particular instant in time and marks a change of state in the system.
Between consecutive events, no change in the system is assumed to occur; thus the simulation time can directly jump to the occurrence time of the next event, which is called next-event time progression. I'm particularly interested in Discrete Event System Specification models (classic DEVS, Cell-DEVS, ...).

Optimization of Complex Systems: In this area, I am focusing on discrete optimization methods (exact, heuristic and meta-heuristic methods) as well as hybrid approaches by coupling different methods: simulation and optimization, multi-criteria decision and multi-objective optimization, exact and approximate methods (meta-heuristics) with the aim of solving large and complex problems.

My application domains are Systems biology and Nanotechnologies.
